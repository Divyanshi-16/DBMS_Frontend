Experiences

Wildlife Safari

No visit to Ranthambhore is complete without a wildlife safari as it is a home to the royal predators ruling the ruggedness of Rajasthan. During a tiger safari in Ranthambhore, you would get to experience the sight of wild cats prancing freely and absorbing the warmth of forest.The tiger is not the only attraction at Ranthambore; although it is the one park resident that people come to see. A variety of birds including Owlets,the ubiquitous langur (monkey), Leapard, Caracal, Hyena, Jackal, Jungle Cat, marsh crocodiles, Wild Boar, Bears and various species of Deer are the other attractions.


Ranthambore Fort

Guests may even visit this magnificant thousand year old UNESCO world heritage sight that offers incredible views of the surrounding forest of Ranthambhore National Park.


Chambal Safari

For the adventure enthusiasts in Ranthambhore, Chambal River is a fascinating tourist spot for sighting amphibians and aquatic animals in the natural habitat. A natural shelter of rare crocodile species "Gharial" and the National Aquatic Animal Gangetic Dolphin, the river is a unique experience, unlike anything on the Indian wildlife circuit. The boat ride on the calm waters of the Chambal, blended with the dramatic landscape and close-up views of the breathtaking abundance of wildlife are the highlights of this adventurous safari.


Festivals of Ranthambore

The Ranthambhore Music & Wildlife Festival is a celebration of music, wildlife, culture & equality. The current edition of the festival is being planned in a special way keeping in mind our beautiful surroundings, hence it is a 'quiet' experience devoid of loud noise. Our artists will be performing acoustic, unplugged sets on all the festival stages.
